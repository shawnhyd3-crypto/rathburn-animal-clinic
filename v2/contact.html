Your first visit

What to expect.

Plan for 30–45 minutes.

First visits aren't rushed. We go through history, do a full physical exam, and leave time for your questions.

Bring records if you have them.

Vaccination history and any recent bloodwork helps us pick up where your previous vet left off. Don't have them? Not a problem — we'll start fresh.

We'll tell you what it costs first.

A standard exam is $95. Anything beyond that gets an estimate before it happens, and you decide whether to proceed.

Parking is free and on-site.

No meters. No garage. No timed permits.
