The practice

A small clinic on Renforth, run the way small clinics used to be run.

Rathburn Animal Clinic has been caring for dogs and cats in Etobicoke since 2004. Dr. Nancy Roncal founded the practice after fifteen years in high-volume hospitals, with a simple idea: slow down. Take the time. Tell people what things cost, before anyone swipes a card.

There is no corporate parent here. No buyout, no brand standards manual, no rotating roster of doctors who won't remember your cat's name. The team that greets you on your first visit is the team that will remember your pet ten years later.

We focus on what small-animal practice does best — wellness and prevention, surgery and dental, same-day urgent care when something goes wrong. For the rare case that needs a specialist, we refer to partners we trust, and we stay involved until your pet is home again.

If that's the kind of vet you're looking for, you're in the right place.

"We're not trying to be everything. We're trying to be the right thing for one animal at a time."
Dr. Nancy Roncal · Founder
02
The doctor

A doctor who answers her own phone.

Dr. Nancy Roncal has been practising veterinary medicine for more than thirty years. She is still the person you are most likely to reach when you call.

Before founding Rathburn, she trained and practised at busy urban hospitals where animals cycled through in fifteen-minute slots. She opened her own clinic in 2004 to do the opposite: thirty-minute first visits, plain-English explanations, honest estimates before anything happens.

First visit

What most people want to know before they come in.

How long will my first visit take?

Plan for thirty to forty-five minutes. We go through full history, do a thorough physical exam, and leave time for your questions. We don't run first visits on a stopwatch.

Do I need to bring anything?

If you have them, vaccination records and any recent bloodwork help us pick up where your previous vet left off. Don't have them? Not a problem — we'll start fresh.

What does a first visit cost?

Standard exam is $95. If vaccines, bloodwork, or other services are needed, we'll give you an estimate before anything happens. You decide what to do from there.

Is parking easy?

Yes. Free on-site parking, just off the 427 at 460 Renforth. No meters, no garage hunt, no timed permits.

Are you taking new patients?

Always, with a wait list during busy seasons. Request a visit online or call us — we'll usually place you within one to two weeks.

What about after-hours emergencies?

We handle same-day urgent care during clinic hours. Outside of those hours, we refer to trusted 24-hour partners in west Toronto — see our emergency page for the full decision tree.
