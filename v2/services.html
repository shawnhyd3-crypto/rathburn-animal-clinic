Wellness & Preventive

Keeping healthy pets healthy.

An annual wellness exam is the cheapest medicine we sell. A thirty-minute visit, head-to-tail physical, and a conversation about diet, weight, behaviour, and anything you've been noticing. Most of what we catch early, we catch at wellness.

Vaccinations are scheduled to your pet's actual risk profile — not a one-size-fits-all protocol. Indoor cats, rural dogs, and city condo dogs don't need the same vaccines on the same schedule.

Dental

The mouth is not cosmetic.

Untreated dental disease affects the heart, the liver, and the kidneys. Most pets over five have some degree of it — usually more than what's visible from the outside.

Our dental cleanings are done under general anaesthesia with full monitoring, digital dental X-rays, and a detailed report home. If extractions are needed, they're done the same day under the same anaesthesia — no second visit, no second bill for it.

·

Dental cleaning + X-rays (no extractions)

from $450
·

Simple extraction (single tooth)

Added to cleaning. Surgical extractions and endodontic work quoted case-by-case.

from $75

Surgery

You'll see the estimate first.

Every surgical procedure gets a written estimate covering anaesthesia, monitoring, medications, and post-op care. You see it, you approve it, and you sign for it before the day of surgery. No exceptions.

For procedures beyond general soft-tissue and dental — orthopaedic work, advanced imaging, specialty surgery — we refer to trusted specialists in west Toronto and stay involved throughout recovery.

Urgent care

Call before you come. We'll be ready.

We hold same-day slots for urgent cases and we don't charge an urgency premium — an urgent exam is the same $95 as a scheduled one. The fastest way in is a phone call; that way we can have the right doctor and the right tools waiting.
