Our Story

Twenty years of quiet consistency.

Dr. Nancy Roncal founded Rathburn Animal Clinic in 2004 after years of practice at a 24-hour hospital in Toronto. The goal was simple: a small, independent clinic where pet owners could get honest answers and unhurried care.

Pet owners should leave every visit well informed and comfortable with the decisions they're making for their pets.

That approach hasn't changed. We stay small, take time with each patient, and stick around long enough that we know your pet by name.

Meet the Veterinarian

Dr. Nancy Roncal

Dr. Nancy Roncal

Veterinarian · Founder

Dr. Roncal has practiced small animal medicine for over 30 years. After earning her Canadian veterinary license, she worked at a 24-hour hospital in Toronto before opening Rathburn Animal Clinic in 2004.

Her approach centers on keeping pet owners well informed and comfortable with the decisions they make about their pets' care. Every visit includes time for questions, explanations, and real conversation about what's best for your animal.

Outside the clinic, Dr. Roncal is married with four children. At home she has a dog and three cats, and she spends her free time gardening, reading, and listening to classical music.
