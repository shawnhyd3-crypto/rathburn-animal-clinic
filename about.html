About Rathburn Animal Clinic

Serving Etobicoke with compassionate, personalized veterinary care since 2004.

Our Story

Rathburn Animal Clinic was founded in 2004 by Dr. Nancy Roncal. With over 30 years of veterinary experience, Dr. Roncal built this independent clinic on the belief that pets deserve individualized care from a team that knows them by name.

We're not a corporate chain. We're your neighborhood clinic, invested in our community and committed to the health and happiness of your pets. Whether you've been with us since 2004 or you're discovering us today, we treat every patient like family.

From routine wellness visits to complex surgeries, from puppies and kittens to senior pets, we've cared for countless Etobicoke families and their animals. That experience has taught us that good veterinary medicine is part science, part art, and entirely about listening to you.

Our Team

Dr. Nancy Roncal

Founder & Veterinarian

Dr. Roncal has practiced small animal medicine for over 30 years. After obtaining her Canadian veterinary license, she worked at a 24-hour hospital in Toronto before opening Rathburn Animal Clinic in 2004.

Her approach centers on keeping pet owners well informed and comfortable with the decisions they make about their pets' care. Every visit includes time for questions, explanations, and real conversation about what's best for your animal.

Outside the clinic, Dr. Roncal is married with four children. At home she has one dog and three cats, and she spends her free time gardening, reading, and listening to classical music.

What Guides Our Care

Independent & Local

Every pet is unique. We listen to your concerns, take time to understand your pet's individual needs, and develop a care plan that works for your family.
