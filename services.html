Services

Full-service care for dogs and cats.

From routine wellness to surgery and diagnostics, we handle most of what your pet needs in one place.

W

Wellness Exams

Annual and semi-annual check-ups. Nose-to-tail physical, weight and dental assessment, diet review, and early detection of issues before they grow.

V

Vaccinations

Puppy and kitten series, boosters, rabies, and lifestyle-based protocols. We'll help you pick what's actually needed.

D

Dental Care

Cleanings under anesthesia, extractions, and home-care coaching.

S

Surgery

Soft tissue, dental, and routine procedures. Full anesthetic monitoring.

N

Spay & Neuter

Safe, age-appropriate procedures with clear recovery instructions.

L

In-House Lab & Diagnostics

Bloodwork, urinalysis, and cytology run on-site for fast results. We also send advanced panels to reference labs when needed.

R

Radiology

Digital X-ray for orthopedic and soft-tissue assessment.

U

Ultrasound

Non-invasive imaging for abdominal and cardiac assessment.

H

Heartworm & Parasite

Annual testing and year-round prevention plans.

E

Urgent & Emergency Care

Same-day appointments for urgent concerns during clinic hours. After-hours cases referred to trusted emergency hospitals in the GTA.

C

Chronic Disease Management

Diabetes, thyroid, kidney, arthritis, and other long-term conditions. Ongoing care with regular check-ins.
