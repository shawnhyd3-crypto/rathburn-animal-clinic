Emergency

When your pet needs help now.

During clinic hours we accept same-day urgent appointments. Outside our hours, please head to an emergency hospital.

During clinic hours: Call us first at (416) 622-8818.

Signs your pet needs urgent care

  • Difficulty breathing or choking
  • Collapse, sudden weakness, or seizures
  • Severe bleeding that won't stop
  • Suspected poisoning (chocolate, xylitol, medications, rodenticide, antifreeze)
  • Trauma: hit by car, fall, bite wound
  • Repeated vomiting or diarrhea (especially with blood)
  • Unable to urinate, especially male cats
  • Bloated or distended abdomen
  • Eye injuries or sudden blindness
  • Heatstroke (panting, collapse, high body temp)

If you're unsure, call us. We'd rather hear from you than have you wait.
