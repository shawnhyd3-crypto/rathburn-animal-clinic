Our Services

We offer a full range of veterinary services to keep your pet healthy and happy.

Wellness Exams

Regular check-ups to monitor your pet's overall health and catch any issues early.

Vaccinations

Protect your pet with essential vaccines tailored to their age and lifestyle.

Dental Care

Professional teeth cleaning and oral health assessment for healthier smiles.

Spay & Neuter

Surgical procedures to prevent unwanted litters and reduce health risks.

Surgery

Advanced surgical care for injuries, conditions, and procedures requiring specialized treatment.

Emergency & Urgent Care

Immediate care for injuries, illnesses, and emergencies when your pet needs us most.

Medical Consultations

Expert guidance on chronic conditions, behavior, nutrition, and wellness strategies.

Radiology

X-ray imaging to diagnose fractures, internal conditions, and other health concerns.

Ultrasound

Advanced imaging to evaluate organs, pregnancies, and soft tissue structures.

Heartworm Testing

Screening to detect heartworm disease and keep your pet protected year-round.

Lab Tests

In-house and external laboratory testing for accurate diagnosis and health monitoring.

Grooming

Professional grooming services to keep your pet clean, comfortable, and looking great.

About Rathburn Animal Clinic

Dr. Nancy Roncal founded Rathburn Animal Clinic in 2004 with over 30 years of veterinary experience. Since then, we've built a reputation as an independent clinic dedicated to personalized, quality care. We know your pets by name and understand what matters most to your family.

Our team works with you to develop a health plan tailored to your pet's individual needs. Whether it's a puppy's first visit or managing a senior pet's care, we're here for every stage of your pet's life.
